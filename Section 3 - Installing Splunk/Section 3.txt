# Phase 1
	1. Navigate to https://www.splunk.com/
	2. Click on "Free Splunk" button.
	3. Create new splunk account if you don't have one. 
	4. After validating you email dashboard will shows up and click "Access instance" button.
	5. You can loigin into cloud dashboard using your email and password. (This is not mandatory)
	

# Phase 3
	1. Navigate to https://www.splunk.com/
	2. Click on Free Splunk. Click on Splunk Enterprise.
	3. Download Windows 64 bit version.
	4. Install Splunk Enterprise on your local machine. Then open it.
	5. Give login creditionals for local server to login to your dash board.
